<p><p>The absolute shaft encoders market is divided into two main types: multi-turn and single-turn encoders. Single-turn encoders capture position within a single rotation, providing high precision but limited overall rotation tracking. In contrast, multi-turn encoders track multiple turns, maintaining position data even when powered off, making them ideal for applications requiring extensive rotation tracking. Both types serve crucial roles in automation, robotics, and industrial applications, ensuring accurate and reliable position measurement across various operational environments.</p></p>
