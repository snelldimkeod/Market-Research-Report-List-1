<p><p>The aesthetic fillers market is primarily divided into absorbable and non-absorbable types. Absorbable fillers, often composed of hyaluronic acid or collagen, provide temporary results, gradually breaking down in the body over time. They are widely favored for their safety and versatility in enhancing facial volume and smoothing wrinkles. Non-absorbable fillers, typically made from substances like silicone or PMMA, offer long-lasting effects but carry higher risks and require careful management. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and treatment objectives in aesthetics.</p></p>
