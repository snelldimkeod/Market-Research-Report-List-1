<p><p>The agricultural irrigation pumps market consists of various types, each serving specific needs. Submersible pumps are designed to operate underwater, effectively lifting water from deep sources. Self-priming pumps can draw water from below their level, making them versatile for various irrigation setups. Vortex pumps are efficient for handling sediment-laden water, preventing clogging and maintaining flow. Additionally, "other" pumps encompass specialized options like centrifugal and linear pumps, catering to diverse agricultural requirements and conditions in irrigation practices.</p></p>
