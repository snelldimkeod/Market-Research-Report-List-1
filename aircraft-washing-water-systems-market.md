<p><p>The aircraft washing water systems market can be categorized into dry and wet systems. Dry systems utilize high-pressure air to remove dirt and debris without water, minimizing environmental impact and optimizing water usage. In contrast, wet systems involve the use of water and detergents to thoroughly clean aircraft surfaces, ensuring effective removal of contaminants. Both types cater to varying operational needs, with dry systems being more suited for eco-conscious facilities, while wet systems deliver deep cleaning for heavily soiled aircraft.</p></p>
