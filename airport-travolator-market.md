<p><p>The airport travolator market consists of two main types: belt type and pallet type. Belt type travolators feature a continuous flat surface, providing a smooth ride for passengers and efficient luggage movement. They are commonly used to transport travelers short distances within terminals. Pallet type travolators have a series of flat platforms, allowing for the movement of larger items and ensuring stability for wheeled luggage. Both types enhance airport efficiency and passenger convenience, promoting seamless transit between terminals and gates.</p></p>
