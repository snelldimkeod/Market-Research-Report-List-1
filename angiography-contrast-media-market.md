<p><ul><li>Organic Iodine Molecules Contrast</li><li>Gadolinium Contrast</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The angiography contrast media market consists of two primary types: organic iodine molecules and gadolinium-based contrast agents. Organic iodine molecules are widely used for X-ray and CT imaging due to their high radiopacity, allowing for clear visualization of blood vessels. Gadolinium contrast agents are primarily utilized in MRI due to their paramagnetic properties, enhancing image quality. Both types play crucial roles in diagnosing vascular conditions, determining treatment plans, and improving patient outcomes in various medical imaging procedures.</p></p>
