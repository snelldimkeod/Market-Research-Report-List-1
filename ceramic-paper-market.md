<p><p>The ceramic paper market is categorized into three main types based on thermal properties. Low thermal conductivity ceramic paper is designed to minimize heat transfer, making it ideal for insulation applications. Low heat storage ceramic paper absorbs less heat, which is useful for protecting sensitive components from overheating. Thermal shock resistance ceramic paper can withstand sudden temperature changes without cracking, making it suitable for high-temperature environments. These properties enhance energy efficiency and durability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
