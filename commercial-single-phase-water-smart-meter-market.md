<p><strong>The Commercial Single Phase Water Smart Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IC Card</li><li>Non-IC Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Single Phase Water Smart Meter market can be divided into two main types: IC Card and Non-IC Card meters. IC Card meters utilize integrated circuit card technology, allowing users to store and manage prepaid water usage seamlessly. These meters enhance data security and facilitate easy recharging. In contrast, Non-IC Card meters operate without this technology, often using traditional methods for monitoring and billing water consumption. Both types serve commercial establishments but cater to different operational and user preferences.</p></p>
