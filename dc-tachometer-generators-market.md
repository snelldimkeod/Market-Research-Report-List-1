<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet Type</li><li>Electromagnetic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC tachometer generators market includes two primary types: permanent magnet and electromagnetic. Permanent magnet tachometers use strong permanent magnets to generate a voltage proportional to the rotational speed, offering higher efficiency and compact design, making them ideal for applications requiring lightweight components. On the other hand, electromagnetic tachometers rely on magnetic fields created by coils and are typically more robust, allowing for higher power outputs and greater durability, making them suitable for industrial applications with varying load conditions.</p></p>
