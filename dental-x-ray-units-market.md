<p><p>The dental X-ray units market comprises two main types: intraoral and extraoral X-ray units. Intraoral X-ray units are designed for imaging within the mouth, allowing dentists to capture detailed views of individual teeth and surrounding structures. Conversely, extraoral X-ray units capture images from outside the mouth, providing broader views, such as panoramic or cephalometric images, useful for orthodontic assessments and treatment planning. Both types are essential for accurate diagnosis and effective dental care, contributing to overall oral health management.</p></p>
