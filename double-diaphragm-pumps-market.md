<p><ul><li>Air Operated</li><li>Electrically Operated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The double diaphragm pump market is primarily divided into two types: air-operated and electrically operated pumps. Air-operated double diaphragm pumps use compressed air to create pressure, making them ideal for transferring viscous fluids and handling abrasive materials. They offer advantages in hazardous environments due to their explosion-proof design. Electrically operated diaphragm pumps, on the other hand, utilize electric motors for fluid movement, providing higher efficiency and control, often suited for applications requiring precise flow rates and less operational noise.</p></p>
