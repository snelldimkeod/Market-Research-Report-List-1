<p><ul><li>Absorption</li><li>Transmission</li><li>Motoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dynamometers measure force, torque, and power in various applications, categorized into three market types. Absorption dynamometers load engines or motors, measuring output power while dissipating excess energy as heat. Transmission dynamometers assess components like gearboxes, monitoring efficiency and performance during operation. Motoring dynamometers serve to test motors by providing a controllable load, simulating real-world conditions to evaluate performance under different scenarios. Each market type plays a crucial role in engineering, automotive testing, and performance validation.</p></p>
