<p><p>The Foundry Additives Market is categorized into organic, inorganic, and hybrid additives. Organic additives are derived from carbon-based compounds, enhancing properties like fluidity and surface finish in foundry processes. Inorganic additives, sourced from minerals and metals, improve strength, heat resistance, and mold stability. Hybrid additives combine characteristics of both organic and inorganic types, offering tailored solutions for specific applications, enhancing overall performance, and addressing diverse casting requirements while optimizing production efficiency in the foundry industry.</p></p>
