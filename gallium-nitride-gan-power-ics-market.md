<p><p>Gallium Nitride (GaN) Power ICs are categorized into two main market types: Depletion Mode and Enhancement Mode. Depletion Mode GaN devices are normally on and require a negative gate voltage to turn off, offering high efficiency for certain applications. In contrast, Enhancement Mode GaN devices are normally off and turn on with a positive gate voltage, making them easier to integrate into existing designs. The choice between these modes influences performance characteristics like switching speed, efficiency, and thermal management.</p></p>
