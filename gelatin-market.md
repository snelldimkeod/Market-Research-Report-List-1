Bone gelatin, extracted from animal bones, finds applications in pharmaceuticals, food processing, and confectionery. Halal gelatin, produced following Islamic dietary laws, caters to the growing demand for food and products compliant with halal standards, ensuring inclusivity for Muslim consumers.</p></p>
