<p><p>The heat exchangers market is primarily segmented into shell and tube, and plate and frame types. Shell and tube heat exchangers consist of a series of tubes, allowing fluid to flow through them, providing efficient heat transfer and high pressure tolerance, ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, plate and frame heat exchangers utilize thin plates to enhance heat transfer efficiency, offering compact designs and ease of maintenance, making them suitable for food processing and HVAC systems. Both types cater to diverse thermal requirements.</p></p>
