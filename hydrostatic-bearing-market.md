<p><p>The hydrostatic bearing market can be segmented into three main types: radial bearings, thrust bearings, and radial thrust bearings. Radial bearings support loads perpendicular to their axis, providing stability and reducing friction in rotating equipment. Thrust bearings, on the other hand, handle axial loads, ensuring smooth operation in applications requiring stability under pressure. Radial thrust bearings combine both types, accommodating both radial and axial loads, making them versatile for various industrial applications, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
