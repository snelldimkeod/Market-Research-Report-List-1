<p><p>The inclinometer market is primarily divided into two types: one-axis and multi-axis inclinometers. One-axis inclinometers measure tilt or inclination in a single direction, making them suitable for applications where only vertical or horizontal orientation is critical. In contrast, multi-axis inclinometers can measure inclination in multiple directions, providing a comprehensive view of an object's orientation. This versatility makes multi-axis devices ideal for complex applications like geological monitoring, structural health assessment, and robotics, where precise measurements in various orientations are essential.</p></p>
