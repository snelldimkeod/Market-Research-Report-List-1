<p><ul><li>Single Stage Pump</li><li>Multiple Stage Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inline process pump market comprises two main types: single-stage pumps and multiple-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed for low to medium pressure applications, delivering fluid in a straightforward manner, making them ideal for tasks requiring efficient and compact solutions. In contrast, multiple-stage pumps utilize two or more impellers to achieve higher pressure levels, making them suitable for transferring fluids over longer distances or to higher elevations. Both types are essential in various industrial processes for optimizing fluid movement.</p></p>
