<p><ul><li>JFET Type</li><li>MOS Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ion Sensitive Field Effect Transistor (ISFET) market is primarily categorized into two types: JFET-based and MOS-based ISFETs. JFET-type ISFETs utilize a Junction Field Effect Transistor structure to detect ion concentration, offering high sensitivity and fast response times. In contrast, MOS-type ISFETs leverage Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor technology, providing enhanced miniaturization and compatibility with integrated circuits. Both types find applications in various fields, including environmental monitoring, biomedical sensors, and industrial processes, with distinct advantages in sensitivity and integration capabilities.</p></p>
