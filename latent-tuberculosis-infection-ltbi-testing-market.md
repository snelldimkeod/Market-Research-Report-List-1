<p><strong>The Latent Tuberculosis Infection (LTBI) Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tuberculin Skin Test (TST)</li><li>Interferon Gamma Released Assay (IGRA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Latent Tuberculosis Infection (LTBI) testing market primarily comprises two types: Tuberculin Skin Test (TST) and Interferon Gamma Release Assay (IGRA). TST involves intradermal injection of purified protein derivative to assess immune response, typically requiring a follow-up reading. In contrast, IGRA is a blood test that measures immune response to specific TB antigens, offering advantages like single visit administration and no reader variability. Both methods are crucial for diagnosing LTBI, impacting public health strategies and treatment approaches.</p></p>
