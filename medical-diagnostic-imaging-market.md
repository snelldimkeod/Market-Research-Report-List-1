<p><p>The medical and diagnostic imaging market comprises various technologies used for visualization of internal body structures. X-rays provide quick images for assessing bone and organ issues. Computed Tomography (CT) combines X-ray technology with computer processing for detailed cross-sectional views. Ultrasound uses high-frequency sound waves for real-time imaging of soft tissues, often in obstetrics. MRI equipment utilizes strong magnetic fields and radio waves for high-resolution images of organs and tissues. Nuclear imaging employs radioactive substances to visualize physiological processes within the body.</p></p>
