<p><ul><li>Forehead Type Thermometer</li><li>Ear Type Thermometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical infrared thermometer market comprises primarily two types: forehead (temporal) and ear (tympanic) thermometers. Forehead thermometers measure temperature by detecting infrared radiation from the skin's surface, providing quick and non-invasive readings, making them ideal for screening. Ear thermometers use infrared sensors to gauge temperature from the tympanic membrane, offering accurate results in a few seconds and being suitable for all age groups. Both types cater to varying patient needs and are essential tools in temperature monitoring.</p></p>
