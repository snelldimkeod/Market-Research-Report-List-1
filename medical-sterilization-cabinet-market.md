<p><p>The medical sterilization cabinet market includes two primary types: bench-top and vertical sterilizers. Bench-top sterilizers are compact, designed for countertop use in smaller clinics and laboratories, offering efficiency without occupying much space. In contrast, vertical sterilizers are larger and often used in hospitals and larger facilities, providing greater capacity and more advanced features for high-volume sterilization processes. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring the safety and sterility of medical instruments and materials.</p></p>
