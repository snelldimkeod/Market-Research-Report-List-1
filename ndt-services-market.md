<p><strong>The NDT Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Testing</li><li>Radiography Testing</li><li>Magnetic Particle Testing</li><li>Liquid Penetrant Testing</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>NDT services encompass various testing methods critical for assessing material integrity. Ultrasonic Testing uses high-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws. Radiography Testing employs X-rays or gamma rays to visualize internal structures. Magnetic Particle Testing identifies surface and near-surface defects through magnetic fields and iron particles. Liquid Penetrant Testing reveals surface-breaking defects by applying a penetrant that seeps into cracks. Other methods include eddy current testing and visual inspections, offering diverse solutions for fault detection in various industries.</p></p>
