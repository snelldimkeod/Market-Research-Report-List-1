<p><p>The non-woven materials market is categorized into two main types: durables and disposables. In contrast, disposable non-woven materials are intended for single-use applications, commonly found in medical supplies, hygiene products, and personal protective equipment. This distinction allows manufacturers to cater to diverse consumer needs, balancing performance with cost-effectiveness in various industries, including healthcare, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
