<p><ul><li>With Spur Gear</li><li>With Spiral Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Parallel Drive Actuator market includes two main types based on gear configuration: with spur gear and with spiral gear. Spur gear actuators utilize straight-toothed gears for precise linear motion, offering high efficiency and reliability in applications requiring straightforward movement. In contrast, spiral gear actuators employ helical gears, which provide smoother operation and higher torque transfer, making them suitable for applications needing quieter operation and reduced backlash. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing automation and precision in movement.</p></p>
