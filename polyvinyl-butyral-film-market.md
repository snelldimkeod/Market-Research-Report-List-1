<p><p>Polyvinyl Butyral (PVB) film is a type of thermoplastic polymer known for its excellent adhesion, optical clarity, and safety features, making it widely used in a variety of applications, particularly in the automotive and construction sectors. Its properties further extend its use in electronic displays, solar panels, and protective equipment.</p><p>The Polyvinyl Butyral Film Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.7% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for safety glass in vehicles and buildings.
