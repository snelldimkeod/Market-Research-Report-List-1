<p><p>The pomegranate powder market features two primary types: spray-dried and freeze-dried pomegranate powder. Spray-drying involves evaporating moisture from pomegranate juice, resulting in a fine powder with a longer shelf life and concentrated flavor, making it suitable for food products and supplements. In contrast, freeze-drying preserves more nutrients and color by removing moisture at low temperatures, yielding a high-quality product ideal for health-conscious consumers. Each type caters to different market needs based on preservation methods and intended applications.</p></p>
