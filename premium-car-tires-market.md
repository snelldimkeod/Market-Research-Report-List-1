<p><strong>The Premium Car Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The premium car tires market primarily consists of two types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature layers of fabric diagonally laid, providing a sturdy but less flexible structure, making them suitable for classic vehicles and off-road applications. In contrast, radial tires have ply cords arranged at 90 degrees to the direction of travel, offering superior performance, improved fuel efficiency, and better handling. Radial tires are the dominant choice for modern vehicles due to their durability and comfort.</p></p>
