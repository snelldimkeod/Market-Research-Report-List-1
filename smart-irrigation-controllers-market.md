<p><p>The smart irrigation controllers market is categorized into two main types: weather-based controllers and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers adjust irrigation schedules based on local weather conditions, utilizing forecasts to optimize water usage. In contrast, sensor-based controllers rely on real-time data from soil moisture sensors or rain gauges to determine when and how much water to apply, ensuring efficient irrigation tailored to the specific needs of the landscape. Both types aim to conserve water and improve irrigation efficiency.</p></p>
