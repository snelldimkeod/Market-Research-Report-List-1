<p><p>The steam dried fishmeal market comprises three main types: defatted fish meal, semi-skimmed fish meal, and whole fish meal. Defatted fish meal undergoes a process to remove lipids, resulting in a protein-rich product mainly used in animal feed. Semi-skimmed fish meal retains some fat, offering a balance of protein and energy, ideal for aquaculture. Whole fish meal contains all parts of the fish, providing high nutritional value, making it suitable for various livestock and aquaculture applications.</p></p>
