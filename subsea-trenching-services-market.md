<p><strong>The Subsea Trenching Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Trenchers</li><li>Jet Trenchers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsea trenching services market involves specialized equipment designed to create trenches on the seabed for various applications, including cable and pipeline installation. Mechanical trenchers utilize rotating blades or chains to excavate the seabed, providing precise control over trench depth and width. In contrast, jet trenchers employ high-pressure water jets to fluidize the sediment, allowing for easier trench formation without disrupting the surrounding environment. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for efficient subsea infrastructure development.</p></p>
