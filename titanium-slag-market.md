<p><ul><li>Acid Soluble Slag</li><li>Chlorination Slag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The titanium slag market is primarily categorized into two types: acid soluble slag and chlorination slag. Acid soluble slag is produced through the high-temperature reduction of titanium ores, yielding a product that can be easily processed with acids to extract titanium. Chlorination slag, on the other hand, is generated using a chlorination process, which involves reacting titanium ores with chlorine to produce titanium tetrachloride. Both types serve as vital intermediates in titanium production, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
