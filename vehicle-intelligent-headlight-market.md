<p><p>The Vehicle Intelligent Headlight Market primarily features two types: Adaptive Front-lighting Systems (AFS) and Adaptive Driving Beam Systems (ADB). AFS enhances visibility by adjusting the headlight beam direction based on vehicle speed and steering angle, improving road illumination during turns. In contrast, ADB optimizes the light distribution by dynamically altering the beam pattern, ensuring maximum visibility without blinding oncoming drivers. Both systems aim to enhance safety and driving comfort by providing better lighting tailored to real-time driving conditions.</p></p>
