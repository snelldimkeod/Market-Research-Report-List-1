<p><p>The ventilator power supply market encompasses various types including AC/DC power supplies, DC/DC power supplies, and internal backup batteries. AC/DC power supplies convert alternating current to direct current, essential for powering ventilators reliably in clinical settings. DC/DC power supplies are used to regulate different voltage levels for specific components of ventilators. Internal backup batteries provide essential power during outages, ensuring continuous operation. Together, these power supplies are critical for the effective functioning of ventilators in healthcare environments.</p></p>
