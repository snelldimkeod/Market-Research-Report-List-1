<p><p>Wire-seal flanges are specialized components used in various industries to ensure secure sealing in critical applications. The Wire Seal Blank Flanges are flat flanges with a wire groove, allowing for custom machining and easy assembly with wire seals. Conversely, Wire Seal Bore Flanges feature a pre-defined bore size, providing a precise fit for specific piping systems. Both types are designed to enhance reliability and prevent leaks, making them essential for pressure-sensitive environments such as oil and gas, aerospace, and chemical processing.</p></p>
