<p><ul><li>RWB Thermal Resistance</li><li>DWB Thermocouple</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless temperature transmitter market comprises three main types: RWB thermal resistance, DWB thermocouple, and other sensors. RWB thermal resistance transmitters utilize resistance change in metals to measure temperature, offering high accuracy for various industrial applications. DWB thermocouples, on the other hand, measure temperature through the voltage generated at the junction of two different metals, making them suitable for extreme conditions. Other market types include Infrared and semiconductor sensors, catering to diverse needs in temperature monitoring across sectors.</p></p>
